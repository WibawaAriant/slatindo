How does the production works?

We buy from the local people the harvested logs directly from the source.

1

Logs are divided into blocks and processed on a scraping table to create wooden slats.

2

This wet wood slat goes through a tapping process before exporting to our customers, drying our wet wood slats in a wood drying furnace service in Jakarta, to prevent blustin

3

Dry wooden slats are bundled in groups of three dozen, placed in sacks, and stored in a Jakarta warehouse, where they await trucking and inspection by Sucofindo officers.
